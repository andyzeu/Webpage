Self-awareness

Collaboration is always a truth teller when it comes to your what you are great at and what you still need to learn.

Scale

Everything can happen when people with different abilities come together and work as a whole. Everyone has a role.

Creative abrasion

Working together with people different than you is a challenge that forces you to show your best.

Take the long view

Even if things do not go well in a collaboration, there are also a lot of things that define a collaboration. So take the good parts.

Repeat

Collaboration is a key factor in learning. Each time you share the work with others you learn a lot or you establish strong connections, or, if you are lucky, both!
